Information for Immigrants from Sri Lanka

If you are an immigrant from Sri Lanka, please make sure you have the following documents and information:

  • Valid passport
  • Visa (if applicable)
  • Travel itinerary
  • Proof of accommodation in India
  • Contact information of local Sri Lankan embassy or consulate
  • Emergency contact information
