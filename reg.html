Immigrant Registration in India

Overview

Immigrants planning to stay in India for an extended period are required to register with the Foreigners Regional Registration Office (FRRO) within 14 days of their arrival in India. Registration is necessary to obtain a residential permit and visa extension, if applicable.

It's essential to follow the registration process diligently to avoid any legal complications during your stay in India.

Documents Required

  • Valid passport with visa
  • Residential address proof
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Completed registration form
  • Any other documents as specified by the FRRO

Ensure that you have all the necessary documents in hand before visiting the FRRO office for registration.

Registration Process

  1. Fill out the registration form available at the FRRO office or online.
  2. Submit the completed form along with the required documents to the FRRO.
  3. Pay the registration fee, if applicable.
  4. Upon verification of documents, the FRRO will issue a residential permit.
  5. Keep the residential permit with you at all times during your stay in India.

Follow any additional instructions provided by the FRRO officers during the registration process.
